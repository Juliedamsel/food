My Favorite Food

My favorite food is an african Nigerian food called bread-fruit popularly known as "Ukwa" by the "Igbo" tribe.

It is a very healthy and nutritious food and contains a lot of nutrients. It is a food highly rich in protein although it contains other nutients such as carbohyydrates,fats & oil,minerals etc. And the interesting part about it is that it is very easy to cook although its can take a while to cook before it becomes perfectly ready to eat..

Ingredients

  • smoked fish
  • Crayfish
  • Palm oil
  • Pepper
  • Salt
  • Maggi Seasoning

How to Prepare Ukwa

  1. Soak the ukwa 20 to 30 mins to help soften it and ready to cook.
  2. wash the ukwa and put in the preferred pot for cooking
  3. cook the ukwa for like 50 to 60 min
  4. Depending on how soft you want your ukwa,you can your ingredients starting with your smoked fish
  5. Add your crafish
  6. add your Pepper
  7. Add the salt followed by your Maggi seasoning
  8. Taste for salt and add if necessary
